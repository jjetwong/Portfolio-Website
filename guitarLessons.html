About Me

I've been playing guitar for more than a decade. Inspiration struck me as a child when AC/DC came roaring out of our car's speakers while on holiday. From that moment I was enamored with the power to create such strong feelings and I felt I'd love to make other people feel the exhiliration that music sparked in me.

I received in-person lessons for 3 years which gave me the strong foundations that I could then build up from. After that I was granted a Music Scholarship at Kingsdale Foundation School where I would receive tuition once a week for the duration of my studies (5 years). After Sixth Form my musical tastes evolved into a wider array of genres including Neo-Soul, Delta Blues and others. I decided to take my Grade 8 Rockschool exam in 2019 as an academic challenge to myself and passed with a Merit soon after.

Now I feel like giving back and helping those who want to become proficient in guitar just like I've done but feel that I can make the journey easier from all the lessons I've learnt along the way.

What you will be learning

If you were to take lessons with me, you would learn a wide range of skills and concepts related to playing the guitar. Some specific things you might learn include:

  • Basic guitar technique, such as how to hold the guitar, how to strum and pick, and how to form chords
  • Music theory fundamentals, such as how to read music notation and understand concepts like scales, intervals, and chord progressions
  • A variety of musical styles and genres, including pop, fingerstyle, blues, rock, funk, jazz, metal...whatever you want!
  • Techniques for playing lead guitar, such as how to play scales, arpeggios, and solos
  • Techniques for fingerpicking and playing acoustic guitar
  • Ways to practice effectively and improve your skills over time

In addition to these technical skills, you would also learn how to express yourself musically through the guitar and use it as a means of self-expression and creativity. I would strive to create a supportive and encouraging learning environment that helps you reach your full potential as a guitar player.
